Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SKYMESS is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal information. You play anonymously.

1. Who We Are

SKYMESS is an air traffic control game developed by BLUESky ("we", "us", "our"). This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use the SKYMESS mobile app or desktop application.

For privacy questions, contact us at: skymess.privacy@gmail.com

2. What Information We Collect

When you launch SKYMESS for the first time, we automatically create an anonymous account for you. This account has no name, no email address, and no password.

Data Purpose Linked to you?
Anonymous user ID (UUID) Identify your account across sessions Yes — but not to your identity
Level progress (current level) Save your game progress Yes
Premium status Determine which levels you can access Yes
Purchase timestamp Record when a purchase was made Yes
Platform (iOS/Android/etc.) Technical compatibility Yes
Advertising ID (free users only) Show relevant ads via Google AdMob Yes

We do not collect: your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, microphone input, or camera data.

4. Advertising (Free Users Only)

If you use the free version of SKYMESS, you will see interstitial ads between levels, provided by Google AdMob. These ads may use your device's advertising identifier to show you relevant ads.

On iOS, we will ask for your permission before showing personalized ads (App Tracking Transparency prompt). You can say no — you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized.

Premium users (who have purchased the full game) do not see any ads.

5. In-App Purchases

SKYMESS offers a one-time purchase to unlock all levels. Purchases are processed by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android). We do not store your payment card details — these are handled entirely by Apple and Google.

RevenueCat is used to verify that a purchase was completed and to update your premium status in our database.

6. Data Retention

Your anonymous account and game data are stored for as long as you use SKYMESS. If you delete your account (via the in-app option in Settings), all your data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Delete — delete your account and all associated data via Settings → Delete Account inside the app, or by contacting us
  • Opt out of personalized ads — decline the ATT prompt on iOS, or opt out via your device's ad settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at: skymess.privacy@gmail.com

8. Children's Privacy

SKYMESS is rated 4+ (Apple) and Everyone (Google Play). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we use anonymous accounts with no personal information, our data practices are compatible with children's privacy requirements. However, SKYMESS is not specifically directed at children.

9. Data Security

All data is transmitted using HTTPS encryption. Our database (Supabase) uses Row Level Security — your data can only be accessed by your own anonymous account. The service_role key that can bypass security is never exposed to client-side code.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of SKYMESS after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
